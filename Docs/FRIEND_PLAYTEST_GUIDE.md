# OPEN PLAN Friend Playtest Guide

Thank you for trying this small, unfinished office-management demo. Please play from the main menu through the first office expansion. There is no countdown and no failure screen, so take your time and manage the workers however feels natural.

## Quick start

- Launch `OpenPlan.exe` and choose **Start Workday**.
- Follow or skip the tutorial.
- Click a worker to inspect them. Hold and drag a worker onto a labeled activity area.
- Desk placement starts Focused Work. Rest, Water, Vending, Smoking, and Leave Office affect different needs and cost work time.
- Earn $1,000, purchase the neighboring unit, hire a worker, and place the hire at a new desk.
- Try the Established Office preview after expanding.
