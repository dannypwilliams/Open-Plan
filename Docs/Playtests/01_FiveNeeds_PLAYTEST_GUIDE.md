# Checkpoint 01 - Five Needs Playtest Guide

Please play for 15-20 minutes and record what actually happens. Do not smooth over confusing moments or rewrite answers more favorably.

## Setup

- Launch `VerifiedExtract/OpenPlan.exe` from the exact extracted Windows package.
- Start a New Campaign.
- Do not use developer tools, test controls, or the Unity Editor.
- Play at 1920x1080 first.
- Repeat the inspector layout check at 1280x720.

## Playtest sequence

1. Confirm the campaign starts with Morgan, Alex, Sam, three desks, and `$0`.
2. Select each employee and inspect all five needs.
3. Explain, without reading documentation, which meters are healthier when full.
4. Let the office run at 1x for two minutes.
5. Record which needs visibly changed.
6. Pause for 30 real seconds and confirm values remain unchanged.
7. Resume at 4x and observe changes.
8. Drag one employee to Rest.
9. Drag one employee near Water.
10. Drag one employee to Vending when affordable.
11. Raise or wait for Bathroom urgency, then place the employee near the restroom.
12. Confirm restroom use lowers the correct meter.
13. Place an employee on ordinary ground and confirm needs persist.
14. Observe an employee working from a desk.
15. If affordable, hire a deskless employee and confirm phone work still functions.
16. Check the employee inspector at 1280x720.
17. Continue long enough to see at least one caution or urgent state.
18. Quit cleanly to the menu or desktop.

## Questions

1. Which three needs are healthier when full?
2. Which two meters represent urgency?
3. Could you tell why an employee was slowing down?
4. Did any need change too quickly?
5. Did any need feel irrelevant?
6. Was the restroom easy to find?
7. Did dropping someone near an activity produce the result you expected?
8. Did the inspector cover too much of the office?
9. Did any text, bar, or icon become unclear at 1280x720?
10. Did you feel forced to micromanage?
11. Which need created the most interesting decision?
12. What was the most confusing moment?
13. Did you encounter a stuck, hidden, or frozen employee?
14. Would you continue into the next checkpoint?
