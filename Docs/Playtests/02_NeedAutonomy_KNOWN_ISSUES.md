# Checkpoint 02 - Known Issues

No P0 or P1 defect is known in the committed automated-acceptance build.

- Grid paths are deterministic and smoothed but may look mechanical at corners; local crowd avoidance and authored locomotion are not implemented.
- Future furniture construction must use the navigation invalidation hook and receive a separate performance pass.
- The restroom is a readable entrance/use point rather than a modeled interior.
- Qualification preference hooks remain neutral until Prompt 03; no qualification pairs or training are active.
- New hires do not automatically claim a later-vacated desk.
- Campaign save/load, workdays/contracts, payroll/reputation, incidents, furniture construction, broader settings/accessibility, animation, and audio production remain future work.
- The deterministic matrix is not a substitute for profiler-grade allocation capture.
- Human playtesting was explicitly waived for this run; no manual acceptance is claimed.
