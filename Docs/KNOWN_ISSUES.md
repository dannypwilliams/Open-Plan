# Known Issues and Limitations

No blocker or critical issue is known for the friend-test build.

## Honest limitations

- The first expansion is the only property purchase in this demo slice.
- There is no save system. Expansion and tutorial progress are session-scoped and reset after returning to the menu or quitting.
- New hires arrive at the entrance and require manual desk placement; there is no onboarding queue.
- Worker movement is direct and deterministic rather than obstacle-aware navmesh pathfinding, so routes can look mechanical.
- Characters use procedural gestures rather than skeletal animation, and audio/Foley variety is limited.
- The vending malfunction is deliberately uncommon, so a short first play may not show it.
- The Starter Office is intentionally open-ended and has no forced loss screen, day-end report, or post-expansion objective beyond continued management and the preview.
- The Established Office is a preserved future-stage sandbox and does not carry the Starter Office's cash or expansion state.
- There is no localization, remappable controls, graphics/settings menu, controller support, or accessibility narration.
- Friend feedback has not yet been collected; this release is ready for that test, not a claim of commercial readiness.

## Current non-goals

Multiple properties, cities, floors, manager roles, rival companies, promotions, relationships, complex finance, and freeform furniture placement remain outside this prototype.
