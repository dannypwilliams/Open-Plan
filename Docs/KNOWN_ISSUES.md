# Known Issues and Limitations

No blocker or critical issue is known for worker-placement pivot checkpoint 7.

## Checkpoint limitations

- The first expansion is intentionally the only property purchase in this demo slice.
- Expansion state persists across an in-session expanded restart, but there is intentionally no save persistence after quitting or returning to the main menu.
- Tutorial progress is intentionally per session; restart begins a fresh tutorial and quitting does not save completion.
- Starter and Established stages are open-ended and have no daily target or workday result screen.
- The Established Office retains direct deterministic steering, procedural character animation, restrained audio, and the task economy as a development/future-stage sandbox.
- There is no save system, localization, remappable controls, or settings menu.

## Current non-goals

- Multiple purchasable properties.
- Multiple cities or districts.
- Multiple floors.
- Managers and specialized roles.
- Rival companies.
- Promotions and relationships.
- Complex finance.
- Furniture-placement mode.
- Save-game persistence.
