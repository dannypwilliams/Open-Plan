# Known Issues and Limitations

No blocker or critical issue is known for Checkpoint `00_Foundation`.

## Honest limitations

- The first expansion is the only property purchase in this checkpoint.
- There is no campaign save system. Expansion, hiring, and tutorial progress are session-scoped and reset after returning to the menu or quitting.
- Live five-need decay/recovery is not active. Happiness, Hunger, Bathroom, Inspiration, and Energy have stable foundational definitions only.
- The 12-strength and 12-liability catalogs and deterministic pair selection exist, but live employees do not receive qualification pairs yet.
- Deskless hires visibly work from phones and take existing restorative breaks; the broader five-need autonomy pass remains future work.
- New hires arrive at the entrance; there is no onboarding queue or automatic desk claim yet.
- Worker movement is direct and deterministic rather than obstacle-aware navmesh pathfinding, so routes can look mechanical.
- Characters use procedural gestures rather than skeletal animation, and audio/Foley variety is limited.
- The vending malfunction is deliberately uncommon, so a short first play may not show it.
- The Starter Office is intentionally open-ended and has no workday, contract, payroll, reputation, incident, furniture-build, forced-loss, or post-expansion objective yet.
- The Established Office is a preserved future-stage sandbox and does not carry the Starter Office's cash or expansion state.
- There is no localization, remappable controls, graphics/settings menu, controller support, or accessibility narration.
- Checkpoint 00 feedback has not yet been collected; this package is ready for a 15–20 minute foundation playtest, not a claim that the 30-day Alpha roadmap is complete.

## Current non-goals

Live five-need recovery, assigned qualifications, skill growth/training, workdays/contracts, payroll/reputation, incidents, furniture build mode, campaign persistence, and final Endless Office Alpha balance/packaging belong to later prompts. Multiple properties, cities, floors, manager roles, rival companies, promotions, relationships, and complex finance remain beyond the current 30-day Alpha scope.
